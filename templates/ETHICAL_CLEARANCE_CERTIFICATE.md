### This clearance is granted on the following understanding

1. No deviation from the approved protocol may be made without prior approval via a Protocol Amendment (Form DREC-06).
2. Any data breach must be reported in writing to the Member Secretary, DREC, within 72 hours (Form DREC-08).
3. An Annual Compliance Declaration (Form DREC-07) must be submitted each academic year that the clearance is active.
4. This ECC number must be cited in the dissertation and in any publication arising from the research.
