How review works

Three review pathways

Fastest

Self-declaration

No primary data and no personal data — literature reviews, meta-analyses, bibliometric and public-domain work.

Near-immediate · Form DREC-02
Light-touch

Expedited

Minimal-risk or exemption-eligible studies — anonymous surveys, public secondary data, licence-only datasets.

~2–3 weeks · Chair + 2 faculty
Full scrutiny

Full board

Sensitive, identifiable, cross-border or dual-use research, or anything above minimal risk.

~4–6 weeks · incl. External Member
